Fair & Transparent Lead Distribution

How we collect and allocate high‑intent leads among pool partners

1. Lead Collection

We aggregate leads from multiple channels – KalkiB2B website, WhatsApp chatbots, social media campaigns, partner referrals, and our AI‑powered outreach engine. Every lead is pre‑qualified.

~5,000+ monthly lead volume (projected by Q4 2026)

2. Intelligent Scoring

Each lead gets an “intent score” (0‑100) based on budget, response rate, project clarity, and industry vertical. Only leads with score ≥70 are routed to partners.

3. Distribution Algorithm

Leads are distributed based on a weighted formula: 40% relevance, 30% capacity, 20% performance, 10% random (fairness).

4. Fairness Guarantees

  • ✓ No single partner receives >15% of leads in 30 days
  • ✓ New partners get 60‑day boosting factor
  • ✓ Partners can set lead capacity limits

Every partner receives leads – not just top performers. We actively rebalance the pool.

How Barter Works in the Pool

Partners exchange services directly or offer expertise to other partners' clients. KalkiB2B charges only 2% of project value (capped at ₹25k).

Example: ₹1L SEO ↔ ₹1L Web Dev. Net cash = ₹0. KalkiB2B fee = ₹2,000 (one side).

White‑label services, matchmaking, and invoicing handled by KalkiB2B.
