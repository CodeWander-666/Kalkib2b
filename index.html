Fair & Transparent Lead Distribution

How we collect and allocate high‑intent leads among pool partners

1. Lead Collection

We aggregate leads from multiple channels – KalkiB2B website, WhatsApp chatbots, social media campaigns, partner referrals, and our AI‑powered outreach engine. Every lead is pre‑qualified by our data science team (budget, timeline, location, service needed).

~5,000+ monthly lead volume (projected by Q4 2026)

2. Intelligent Scoring

Each lead gets an “intent score” (0‑100) based on budget, response rate, project clarity, and industry vertical. Only leads with score ≥70 are routed to partners – no spam, no low‑intent inquiries.

3. Distribution Algorithm

Leads are distributed based on a weighted formula:
40% relevance (partner’s primary service categories + location match)
30% capacity (current open leads / project load)
20% performance (historical conversion rate, response time)
10% random (to ensure fairness and opportunity for new partners).

4. Fairness Guarantees

  • ✓ No single partner can receive more than 15% of total leads in a 30‑day window.
  • ✓ New partners get a “boosting factor” for first 60 days to ensure they receive opportunities.
  • ✓ Partners can set lead capacity limits (e.g., max 10 leads/week).
  • ✓ Full transparency: each lead comes with a score & routing reason visible in dashboard.

Every partner receives leads – not just top performers. We actively rebalance the pool.

How Barter Works in the Pool

Partners can exchange services directly or offer their expertise to other partners' clients. KalkiB2B charges only 2% of the project value (capped at ₹25k) from the receiving agency.

Example: Agency A provides ₹1,00,000 SEO to Agency B. Agency B provides ₹1,00,000 web development to Agency A. Net cash = ₹0. KalkiB2B commission = ₹2,000 (only one side).

You can also offer white‑label services to other partners – they sell to their clients, you deliver, and both earn. KalkiB2B handles the matchmaking and invoicing.
