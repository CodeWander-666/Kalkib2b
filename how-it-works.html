1

Partner Onboarding & Profile Setup

After signing up, you complete a detailed profile: primary service categories (SEO, PPC, web dev, AI, etc.), geographic focus (you can serve pan‑India or specific cities), capacity limits (max leads per week/month), and minimum project budget. This data feeds directly into our distribution algorithm.

  • Verified MSME / agency credentials (GST, Udyam, etc.)
  • Portfolio & case studies upload (optional, boosts trust score)
  • Barter preferences – what services you offer vs. what you need
Partner tier (Pilot / Scale / Enterprise) determines lead volume and priority in distribution. Higher tiers receive more leads and earlier access.
2

Lead Collection & AI Intent Scoring

We aggregate leads from multiple channels: the KalkiB2B website, WhatsApp chatbots, social media ads, partner referrals, and our proprietary outreach engine. Every lead is then processed by our data science team (100+ analysts) and an ML model that assigns an Intent Score (0–100) based on:

  • • Budget range & clarity
  • • Response time to our qualification questions
  • • Project timeline (urgency)
  • • Industry vertical and service match

Only leads with Intent Score ≥70 enter the distribution pool – zero spam, zero low‑quality inquiries.

3

Weighted Lead Distribution – Fairness Algorithm

Every qualified lead is routed to the most suitable partner using a real‑time weighted formula. No manual bias, no permanent advantage to any single partner.

40% Relevance

Match between lead’s service requirement and partner’s primary categories + geographic proximity (if location‑specific).

30% Capacity

Partner’s current open leads vs. self‑set capacity. Partners with lower load get higher priority.

20% Performance

Historical conversion rate, average response time, and client feedback. High performers get a slight boost, but never dominate.

10% Random

Ensures that even new or lower‑volume partners receive opportunities. Prevents algorithm gaming.

Fairness Guarantee: No single partner receives more than 15% of total leads in any 30‑day window. New partners get a 60‑day boosting factor (+20% weight) to accelerate their pipeline.

4

Lead Acceptance & Conversion – Pay Only 2%

When a lead is assigned, you receive a notification (WhatsApp + email + dashboard). You have 24 hours to accept or reject. If rejected, the lead is instantly rerouted to the next best partner.

After acceptance, you contact the prospect directly. KalkiB2B does not interfere in negotiations. Once the deal is closed, you mark it as “Converted” in the dashboard. We then deduct a 2% success fee (capped at ₹25k per project) – only on the value you receive (if it's a barter transaction, the fee is based on the market value of the service).

Success Fee Examples

  • • ₹1,00,000 cash project → fee = ₹2,000
  • • ₹5,00,000 cash project → fee = ₹10,000 (still only 2%)
  • • Barter swap (₹1L service ↔ ₹1L service) → fee = ₹2,000 from one side
  • • No conversion → zero fee, zero charges
5

Barter & White‑Label Collaboration

One of the most powerful features: you can exchange services with other partners without paying cash. Use the barter marketplace to:

Direct Swap

Agency A SEO ↔ Agency B web dev. 2% fee on market value.

White‑Label Fulfillment

You sell a service you don't have in‑house to your client, we connect you with a partner who delivers. You keep majority margin.

Credit System

Barter credits earned can be used to pay subscription fees or future commissions.

KalkiB2B acts as a trusted mediator – we ensure quality, timelines, and dispute resolution.

6

Real‑Time Dashboard & Analytics

Every partner gets a personalized dashboard showing:

  • • Leads received – with intent score, budget, and distribution reason
  • • Accepted / rejected / converted – conversion rate, average response time
  • • Barter credits balance – incoming and outgoing
  • • Commission summary – paid and pending
  • • Performance score – compared to pool average

API access also available for larger partners to pull lead data directly into their own CRM.

Additional Rules & Quality Assurance

Lead Return Policy

If a lead is unresponsive or clearly misrepresented, you can return it within 7 days – no penalty, and the lead is reassigned.

Dispute Resolution

KalkiB2B mediates any disagreements between partners or with end clients. We have a binding arbitration process to ensure fairness.

Quality Audit

We randomly audit 10% of closed projects to ensure client satisfaction. Poor ratings can temporarily reduce lead allocation.
