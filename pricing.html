How the 2% Success Fee Works

What you pay

Only when a lead converts into a paid project. 2% of the project value (capped at ₹25,000 per project). If the deal is barter, the fee is calculated on the fair market value of the service exchanged.

Example: You close a ₹5,00,000 SEO contract → success fee = ₹10,000 (2%). You close a ₹1,00,000 web dev project → fee = ₹2,000.

What you never pay

  • No upfront lead fees
  • No hidden charges
  • No commission if you don't close
  • No penalty for rejecting leads
Success fee is deducted automatically from the payment gateway when you mark a lead as “converted”. You can also pay via barter credits.

Barter Credits – The Currency of Collaboration

Every active partner earns monthly barter credits based on their plan (₹20k for Pilot, ₹75k for Scale). These credits can be used to:

  • Pay success fees (instead of cash)
  • Purchase services from other partners (SEO, web dev, design, etc.)
  • Reduce your monthly subscription fee
Unused credits roll over for up to 3 months. You can also earn extra credits by referring new partners or by delivering high‑quality leads.

Example

A Scale partner has ₹75k credits. They need a ₹1,00,000 web development service from another partner. They use ₹75k credits + pay ₹25k cash. The success fee for the transaction is only 2% of the service value (₹2,000) – which can also be paid from credits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, anytime. Changes reflect from the next billing cycle.

What happens if I don't use all leads?

Unused leads do not roll over, but you can adjust your capacity settings to receive fewer leads.

Is there a setup fee?

No. Zero setup cost.

How is the success fee collected?

Via Razorpay – you mark the deal as "converted", and the fee is deducted automatically from the payment you receive (or you can pay separately).

Can I pay subscription via barter credits?

Yes, after 3 months of active partnership you can use up to 50% of credits towards subscription.

What if a lead turns out to be low quality?

Return the lead within 7 days – no penalty. It will be reassigned.
